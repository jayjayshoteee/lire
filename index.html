My dearest Tofunmi,

Ìfẹ́ rẹ̀ ṣe mí ni kó o ṣeéṣe fún mi (Your love makes it possible for me) to see the world in the most beautiful light. O nífẹ̀ẹ́ rẹ̀ gẹgẹ bí òru, ìmọ́lẹ̀ ní kó o fi gbogbo àwọn àyè (Your love is like the night, illuminating every space). Each day with you is like a new dawn.

You are the melody that makes my heart sing, the gentle whisper in my soul that reminds me of what truly matters. O jẹ́ ẹ̀dá kan, ìtàn ìfẹ́ tí kò ní parí (You are a creation, a love story that will never end). With every breath, I find myself more deeply intertwined with you.

As we continue to journey together, I cherish each moment we share. Mo nírètí pé a ó fi ìrìn àjò wa kọ ìtàn pẹ̀lú (I hope we will write our story together) and build countless beautiful memories. Ìfẹ́ rẹ̀ ni gbogbo ohun tí mo fẹ́ (Your love is everything I desire). Your presence is my greatest gift.

Ìfẹ́ rẹ̀ jẹ́ ọlọ́rọ̀ àti onígbọ̀ràn (Your love is rich and nurturing). Thank you for being the wonderful person you are, and for filling my life with so much happiness. I look forward to every moment with you and all the adventures we will embark upon together.

Thank you for being my everything.
